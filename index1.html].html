International Mother Language Day 21st February






The language movement of our country is a very important even in our history and as well as the world. There is no nation in history that has shed blood for its mother tongue. It is matter of pride and great honour to Bangladesh as UNESCO has declared 21st February as the International Mother Language Day.






What is the language movement?





The language movement took place in 1952 in the East Pakistan. People organized the movement demanding the Bangla should be declared the official language. On 21st February 1952, police fired at the rally and killed dozens of people including Abul, Salam, Abdul. This eventually led to the war of 1972.
